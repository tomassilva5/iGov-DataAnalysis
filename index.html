The resolution rate is the strongest predictor of satisfaction (coeff. +0.04). Investing in agent training and decision support tools can increase first-contact resolution.
Reduce Response Time
An average response time of 60 minutes is high. Automation with chatbots and smart prioritization can reduce this value and improve the citizen's experience.

The Decision Support System allows simulating different operational scenarios before implementing changes. Use it to evaluate the expected impact of improvements before applying them.
